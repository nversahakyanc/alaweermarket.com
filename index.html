About Us

Al Aweer Fruits and Vegetable market

Welcome to the vibrant world of Al Aweer Fruits and Vegetables Market, nestled in Ras Al Khor, Dubai UAE. Spanning over a million square meters, this market is not just a place to shop; it's an experience unlike any other. Let's embark on a journey to discover the magic behind this culinary oasis. Step into the bustling Al Aweer Fruits and Vegetables Market, where freshness thrives, and flavors dance on your palate. With over 20,000 visitors daily, this market is more than just a shopping destination; it's a cultural melting pot of gastronomic delights.

At the core of Al Aweer Market lies a commitment to freshness. With 7000 metric tons of fresh produce distributed daily, it's no wonder this market is the pulse of Dubai's culinary scene. For food enthusiasts, Al Aweer Market is paradise. With over 1000 wholesalers and 1500 retailers, it offers a treasure trove of fruits, vegetables, and culinary delights from around the globe.

Diversity is the cornerstone of Al Aweer Market. With produce sourced from over 20 major exporting countries, it caters to Dubai's multicultural population of 200 nationalities. Logistics are seamless at Al Aweer Market. With facilities to accommodate 800 shipping containers and over 2000 truck parking slots, freshness is guaranteed from farm to table.

Al Aweer Market is more than just a marketplace; it's a bridge that connects cultures. From Asian spices to European delicacies, it's a celebration of culinary diversity.
